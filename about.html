About Us

Hotel Satyam Shirdi is spread across 2.5 acres of land surrounded with spectacular views of Fields & lush green tranquility, with Birds Chirping waking you up every morning is located 9 Kms before Shirdi Sai Temple on Mumbai Road. Hotel Satyam Shirdi is the only 11 Room Boutique Resort with Swimming pool In Shirdi. At Hotel Satyam you have different types of accommodation options to choose from Luxurious & Spacious Rooms and Suites all with Pool View. All units are well appointed with classic interiors and amenities, to provide you the most pleasant experience in this pilgrimage station.

A Pool Facing Restaurant which serves delicious Gujarati/Marwari Food and a multi cuisine A La Carte Restaurant with in room dining facilities. An Open Air garden restaurant which serves Delicious Local Specialty and Tea for that lazy afternoon. A Half Olympic size swimming pool with 5 feet depth to enjoy the dip in the water. No baby Pool, parent’s to accompany child below 10 years as no life guard.

The Resort also has Indoor/Outdoor games consisting of Carom, Chess, Ludo, Board games, Badminton, Volleyball, Football, Seesaw, Slides and many other activities to unwind. From the Pool side Lawns spread over 5000 Sq Ft to Big Size wedding lawns spread over 15000 Sq Ft we at Hotel Satyam provide personalized and innovative services and complete solution to our guests for their special day may it be Birthday, Engagement, Wedding or any other special moment.
